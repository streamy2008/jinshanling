Winter Solstice in the Alps

Captured during the blue hour at the peak of Mount Pilatus. The silence of the snowfall was profound, creating a monochromatic world where time seemed to suspend itself. This frame represents the isolation and purity of the winter season.

Camera Leica M11
Lens Summilux 35mm f/1.4
Aperture f/5.6
Shutter 1/250s
ISO 64

Sean

The Vision
of Nature

Sean Image is a digital curator and editorial photographer whose work navigates the delicate interplay between raw landscapes and the passage of time. Based in the Pacific Northwest, his practice is defined by a rhythmic observation of the four seasons.

His photographs are more than mere captures; they are curated magazine spreads of the natural world. By prioritizing the silence within a frame, Sean transforms standard landscape photography into a high-end gallery experience, where every light leak and seasonal shift is treated with architectural precision.

Focus Ephemeral Landscapes
Medium Digital Medium Format
Location Worldwide
